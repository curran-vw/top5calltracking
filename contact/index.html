Contact

Email me

No contact form. I keep one inbox and try to read everything that comes in.

If you've spotted a stale price, a feature change I missed, or a fact I got wrong, please tell me. I'd rather correct the page than leave it standing. I usually respond within two business days.

Email: lisa@top5calltracking.com

What I'll act on

  • Pricing changes (a tool moved a price, added a tier, changed billing cadence)
  • Feature changes (a platform shipped or removed something I describe)
  • Factual errors (I got a number, name, or mechanism wrong)
  • A tool I should review and haven't yet (especially scrappy alternatives)
  • Broken links

What I won't do

  • Take payment in exchange for a higher rank or a positive review. The credibility of this site is worth more than that.
  • Post sponsored content as editorial. If I ever do an actual sponsored piece, it will be clearly marked.
  • Add a tool to the top 5 just because the team behind it asked nicely. I have to use it on real client work first.

If you want to verify my testing

The test campaigns, landing pages, and per-platform setup logs are available on request. Email me with the URL of the page you're checking and what you'd like to see. I'm happy to share what I can without exposing client data.

No contact form by design (it keeps spam off the inbox). Email is the fastest way to reach me.
